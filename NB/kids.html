Kids Band

Kids all want to be in bands but that usually means playing other people's songs.

Lots of people can sing but song writing is a quite different skill. With basic principles I can show kids how to perform and record original material which is within their technical limits.

Even with only a few individuals I can fill in any instrument that's missing from the line up so there is quickly a feeling of being in a band.

I try to create a sense of direction ..building up towards fun and informal gigs for parents, grandparents and friends.
