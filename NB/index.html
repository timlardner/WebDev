About Me

I am a graduate in philosophy from Edinburgh University. In my twenties and thirties I worked as a professional performer in music, dance and theatre. I was the singer/songwriter for a rock band that achieved considerable local and national success.

The band toured with Jools Holland and enjoyed a lot of airplay and good press. We were known for our musicianship and song writing.

I now write songs for other younger acts and work as a voice-over artist.

I have many years of teaching experience with young bands and individuals and have taught song writing and composition both as a night class for The City of  Edinburgh Council and also in a classroom situation.
