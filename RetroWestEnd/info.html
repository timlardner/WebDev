Retrosexual @ The Viper- 500 Great Western Road, Glasgow (West End).

Launched march 2011,

Retrosexual invades once a month, firing out the best indie/electro/house all night.

With cheap booze, good banter and interesting antics- it’s been a great night so far. Don’t just take our word for it, come check it out.

An eclectic combination of the best Indie & house music around. Featuring a spotlight on upcoming talent with EP showcasing from 7pm, from classic to current Indie played from 9pm and dancefloor filling electrohouse through to dubstep from 11pm.

* * * * DJs PMcQ & Frazer MacRobert * * * *

Let PMcQ (Muck) get you moving: The last year has seen him play at and promote nights with dj’s like Kissy Sell Out, Lee Mortimer, Malente, Strip Steve, Mightyfools, Reset!, Clouds, Yolanda Be Cool and Burns. The success of these events has lead to slots at festivals across the UK including T in the Park, Rockness, Kendal Calling and Wizard Festival.

Also getting the night underway Frazer MacRobert (Suck My Deck) will be bringing the night in and getting you in the mood.
