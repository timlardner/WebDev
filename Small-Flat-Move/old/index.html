Welcome to Intransit on the World Wide Web

INTRANSIT is an Edinburgh-based van and hand removal service which has quickly established a

reputation for reliability and quality since we began helping people to move in 1991.

 

 

Our customers typically want to move, boxes, bags and a few items of furniture.

We send round one of our friendly, experienced drivers who will help out with loading.

There are no hourly or mileage charges, just a simple fixed rate of

£47 for a van-load.

Although most of our work is in Edinburgh, we do help people move to many parts of Scotland and

Northern England.

 

For more detailed information on the range of Intransit services click on either Local or National links

then contact us and we can discuss your move.

 

 

 

Most of our work comes from word of mouth but you will also find INTRANSIT in

The List and the Yellow Pages.

 

 

If you want to arrange storage or buy boxes and packing materials we are happy

to recommend other businesses who will help with these additional areas.

You'll find all their details on our Links Page.
