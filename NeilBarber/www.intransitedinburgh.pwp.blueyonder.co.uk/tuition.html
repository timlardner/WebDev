Tuition

Learning the guitar is different from some other instruments because after learning the basics, every player's style is different and technical improvement is driven by the player's own ambition and plans for him/herself.

It is therefore very important that lessons are fun right from the start and that students learn to play on songs they know and like. I use bass guitar and drum machine when teaching so that students quickly enjoy a sense of being in a band.
